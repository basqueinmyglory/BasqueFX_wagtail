About This Site

BasqueFX serves several purposes. The first is to gain experience with python. This site was built using Django and a postgres database. I plan to add other features to this site. It really all depends on what I decide to learn next. BasqueFx is essentially a place for me to display my python projects. The second is that I want a place to store and display thoughts on the forex markets.

I have a Masters Degree in Economics so naturally I find the forex markets interesting. Specifically fundamental analysis. I find technical trading to be robotic and unsatisfying. I need to point out that I have very limited experience with forex trading. This is a place for me to store my ideas. If you happen to stumble across my site, please do not use anything I say in your trading. I'm not giving out trading advice, I'm just recording my thoughts.
